Nature and Nurture

"Play as a snake who stumbles upon a town of talking mushrooms who help you connect yourself with nature."

Cesar Morales-Matute (HS junior)

Game Developer in the Making

PITCH

In Nature and Nurture, you play as a snake who has gotten lost in a previously undiscovered town of talking mushrooms. They have magical abilities and can sense your connection to nature, of which you are lacking. In my game, the mushroom people put you through a course to try and connect you with nature. Throughout the game, you do many quests which might include cleaning up litter, delivering something for the people, etc. Use your weapons along with your heart to connect yourself with nature.

THEME

The beginning of the game starts with you discovering something new about nature, which in real life usually inspires people to get more involved and connected. The mushroom people talk about ways to get yourself more connected, and they make sure to help you out in whatever way they can. The game’s environment connects to the theme because you are in the middle of the forest, forcing you to experience what it’s really like. The player does many quests for the people, not only helping nature out but also themselves.

MY WHY

I was inspired about this theme because I realized that a lot of nature is neglected and looked over. There are still so many things we don't know about our planets but we are already moving on to others. I think we should focus on appreciating our own planet and discovering more but without disturbing the peace. Many smaller ecosystems and even some larger ones have been devastated by humans' interference. I think the best thing we could do is observe from afar while still appreciating nature.
